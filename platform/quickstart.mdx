This guide walks you through creating your first social wall in SocialMatix—from connecting a source to embedding the wall on your website.

## Before you begin

- A SocialMatix account
- Login credentials for at least one social account you want to display (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
- Access to your website's code or CMS so you can paste an embed snippet

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a source to your feed">
    Open **My First Feed** and click **Add Source**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a social network">
    Select a source from the list (for example, **Instagram**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your account">
    Add your account and follow the OAuth prompts to authorize SocialMatix. Once authorized, your posts are ingested automatically and become available in the feed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and style your wall">
    Review the ingested posts. Edit content, hide posts, and adjust styling until the wall looks the way you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Click **Publish Changes** to save your feed and make it live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your embed code">
    Click **Get Code** to copy the embed snippet, then paste it into your website wherever you want the social wall to display.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Newly published changes can take a few moments to propagate to embedded walls. Refresh your site to see the latest version.
</Note>
